MINESWEEPER!



Click on a random square while avoiding the explosives. If the first player clicks on a safe area, the square will either be blank or will contain a number from 1 to 8 showing how many explosives are near that particular square. Your mission if you decide to accept it is to expose all blank sqaures. If you fail the mission is over.

Take turns with a friend for extra suspense. Hit your respective button after each turn. The player with the most points at the end of game wins!
